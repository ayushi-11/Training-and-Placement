The Training and Placement Cell located at JIIT Noida centrally handles campus placement of the graduating students of all our campuses, namely JIIT Noida, JUIT Waknaghat, JUET Guna and Jaypee Business School. The Cell provides complete support to the visiting companies at every stage of placement process. Arrangements for Pre-Placement Talks, Written Tests, Interviews and Group Discussions are made as per the requirement of the visiting companies . The T&P is headed by Cmde KK Rohatgi ,VSM (Retd) , Dean Students and supported by Sr Placement Officers Mrs Anita Marwaha and Anurag Srivastava at Noida, A dedicated Trg and Placement Officer Mr Dig Vijay Chauhan at JUET Guna and Faculty in charge Dr Rahul Shrivastava at JUIT Waknaghat further augment the efforts of T&P. Full placement support to our new University at Anupshahr commissioned in 2014 will also be provided to the graduating students. Our students graduating in 2015 from of all our three Universities , have received offers from national and multi-national companies with CTC varying from 3.4 lac to 24 lac . We are proud to share that our 1400 eligible students have achieved 2300 offers as on date. Of the 53 companies which visited us , 23 have offered CTC > Rs 5 lac.
